## 1. Problem Statement
Recruiters often receive many resumes, and reviewing them manually takes time and may lead to inconsistent results. This project aims to provide an automated 
solution that can analyse resumes quickly and give structured, unbiased feedback to support better decision-making.

## 2. Project Scope
- Upload resumes in PDF, DOC, or DOCX formats  
- Extract and clean text from resumes  
- Detect key technical and soft skills  
- Estimate work experience from date ranges  
- Generate a resume score and suggestions  
- Display all results through a simple web interface  

## 3. Target Users
- ### Students and Freshers 
  Users who want quick feedback on their resumes before applying for jobs or internships.

- ### Job Seekers  
  Individuals looking to improve the quality of their resumes and understand their strengths and missing skills.

- ### Recruiters
  Recruiters who need a quick initial overview of a resume before conducting a detailed manual review.

## 4. High-Level Features

- **Resume Upload** for PDF, DOC, and DOCX files.  
- **Text Extraction** from uploaded resumes.  
- **Skill Detection** using a predefined skill list.  
- **Experience Estimation** based on dates in the resume.  
- **Scoring and Suggestions** to help users improve their resume.
